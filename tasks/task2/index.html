Simple Process

Counsulting

Client Meeting: We begin by meeting with the client to understand their needs, preferences, and vision for the space. This is a crucial step where we establish a clear understanding of the project scope, budget, and timeline.

Site Assessment: Our team conducts a thorough assessment of the space, taking measurements and noting any architectural features or structural considerations that may impact the design.

Concept Development: Based on the information gathered from the client meeting and site assessment, we develop initial design concepts that reflect the client's style and objectives. This may include mood boards, sketches, and 3D renderings to help visualize the proposed designs.

Start Over

Design Refinement: After presenting the initial concepts to the client, we gather feedback and collaborate closely to refine the design. This stage may involve multiple rounds of revisions until we achieve a design that meets the client's vision and expectations.

Material Selection: Once the design is finalized, we work with the client to select materials, finishes, furnishings, and accessories that complement the overall aesthetic and functional requirements of the space.

Procurement: We handle the procurement process, sourcing and purchasing all necessary materials and furnishings while ensuring quality and staying within budget constraints.

Dream HandOver

Execution: With the design and materials finalized, our team begins the construction and installation process. We work closely with trusted contractors and craftsmen to bring the design to life, overseeing every aspect of the project to ensure quality and adherence to the design plan.

Final Touches: As the project nears completion, we add the final decorative touches and accessories to enhance the overall look and feel of the space.

Client Handover: Once the project is finished, we conduct a final walkthrough with the client to ensure their satisfaction with the completed design. We provide guidance on how to care for and maintain their newly designed space, as well as any warranties or guarantees on the workmanship and materials used.
